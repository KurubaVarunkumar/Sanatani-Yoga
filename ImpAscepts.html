Imp Aspects

Yoga is a holistic system that originated in ancient India and has been practiced for thousands of years. It encompasses a variety of physical, mental, and spiritual practices aimed at promoting overall well-being and harmony. The word "yoga" itself comes from the Sanskrit word "yuj," which means to yoke or unite, reflecting the union of body, mind, and spirit that yoga seeks to achieve.
